# 🚀 SignalIQ: Commercial Overview

**Bottom Line Up Front:** SignalIQ does not predict the future. It measures the exact distance between public narrative and actual capital flows. For institutional investors, this distance is where risk is born.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Positioning

In today's markets, information is not a competitive advantage — it is a distraction.

Institutional investors do not suffer from a lack of data, but from an excess of noise. Every day, thousands of headlines shape market perception, creating narrative currents that often run completely counter to what is actually happening in the order books.

**SignalIQ** was built to solve one critical problem:

> *"Is the market still believing its own story?"*

The platform systematically measures divergence between media enthusiasm and price behavior. It does not guess absolute prices. It measures risk conditions.

## 📊 The Four Risk Regimes

SignalIQ classifies any asset or sector into four tactical regimes based on the relationship between narrative intensity and price action.

### 🔬 Concrete Example: NVDA – March 2024

| Phase | Headlines | Price action | SignalIQ diagnosis |
|-------|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Start | "Unstoppable AI rally," "New ATH incoming" | Climbing | 🟢 Aligned |
| Mid | Same euphoria continues | Flat for 11 days | 🟡 Narrative Exhaustion → 🟠 Divergence Warning |
| End | — | 9% drawdown | 🔴 Outcome confirmed |

### The Four Regimes

| Regime | Signal | What is happening | Historical tendency |
|--------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 🟢 Aligned | Narrative = Price | Healthy trend. Media and capital flows agree. | Low risk, sustainable |
| 🟡 Narrative Exhaustion | Narrative > Price | Optimism persists. Momentum weakens. Running out of new buyers. | Trend deceleration |
| 🟠 Divergence Warning | Narrative ↑↑ Price ↓ | Institutional money quietly exiting. Public still buying the hype. | 80%+ of corrections start here |
| 🔴 Critical Divergence | Narrative extreme, Price breaking | Mathematically extreme dislocation. | Precedes abrupt 10%+ drops |

> **For the Investment Committee:** Divergence warnings do not mean "sell now." They mean the risk surface has changed. Verify positioning.
