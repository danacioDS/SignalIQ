# SignalIQ

SignalIQ is an AI-powered Narrative Intelligence platform that analyzes financial news, market narratives, sentiment shifts, and price behavior to identify divergence signals before they become consensus.

The platform continuously ingests market information, extracts narrative drivers, measures divergence between sentiment and market momentum, and produces explainable investment signals.

Key capabilities:

* Real-time financial news analysis
* Narrative Divergence Index (NDI)
* Explainable signal generation
* Historical validation and backtesting
* Multi-LLM architecture
* Cloud-native deployment

SignalIQ is designed to help investors detect when market narratives and price action diverge, creating opportunities before broader market recognition.
